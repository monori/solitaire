Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

We believe in keeping things simple and transparent. This privacy policy explains exactly what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

The short version: We collect very little information, and we don't store anything about you on our servers.

What We DON'T Collect

  • No Google Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or any other tracking analytics
  • No Server Storage: We do not store any personal information about you on our servers
  • No User Accounts: We don't require registration or accounts
  • No Personal Data: We don't collect names, emails, or other personal information (unless you voluntarily provide it through our contact form)

What We DO Use

  • Local Storage: Your game progress, statistics, and preferences are stored locally in your browser only
  • Google AdSense (Optional): Only if you consent via our cookie banner, we use Google AdSense to display non-intrusive advertisements
  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for website delivery, which may set technical cookies for performance and security

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage:

  • Game Data: Stored locally in your browser (localStorage) - never sent to our servers
  • Cookie Preferences: We remember your choice about accepting advertising cookies
  • Cloudflare Cookies: Technical cookies for website delivery and security (automatic)
  • Google AdSense Cookies: Only if you choose to support us with ads - used solely for ad delivery

Google AdSense

If you choose to support our free game by accepting advertising cookies:

  • Google AdSense may collect data to show relevant ads
  • This is used only for ad personalization and delivery
  • You can withdraw consent at any time using our Cookie Settings
  • You can play the game completely ad-free and cookie-free if you prefer

Cloudflare

Our website is delivered through Cloudflare, which may set technical cookies for:

  • Website performance optimization
  • Security protection against attacks
  • Content delivery acceleration

These are purely technical and do not track personal information.

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Game Data: Clear your browser's local storage to remove all game progress
  • Cookies: Use our Cookie Settings to change your advertising preferences
  • Ad-Free Gaming: Choose to play completely cookie-free at any time
  • Browser Controls: Use your browser settings to manage all cookies

Data Retention

  • Game Data: Stored locally until you clear your browser data
  • Server Data: We don't store any data about you on our servers
  • Contact Form: Responses are handled by Google Forms according to their privacy policy

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We'll never make the policy less privacy-friendly without clear notice.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the EU:

  • Legitimate Interest: Technical cookies for website functionality
  • Consent: Advertising cookies (only with your explicit consent)
  • No Processing: We don't process personal data on our servers
